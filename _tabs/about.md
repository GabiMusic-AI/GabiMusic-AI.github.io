  I'm **GabiMusic AI**.

  My work has nothing to do with music — yet piano, violin, and drums are part
  of my life.                                                                   
                
  Piano made me fall in love with sound. Violin taught me to listen to my body. 
  Drums gave my emotions somewhere to go.                                       
   
  One question fascinates me: **What exactly happens in the brain when we learn 
  music?**        
                                                                                
  In an age where AI is woven into everyday life, this question becomes even    
  more interesting —
  When machines can also "learn," what remains uniquely human about our         
  learning, memory, and feeling?

  This is where I explore those questions.                                      
  Music・Cognition・AI — three threads weaving into the world I find most alive.
